### Long Description (1500 characters max)

New Year Countdown is a beautifully designed screensaver for your Roku device that displays a real-time countdown to midnight on New Year's Eve.

**Features:**
• Live countdown in days : hours : minutes : seconds format, updating every second
• Elegant dark navy background with gold accent lines and subtle sparkle animation
• Automatically detects the current year and counts down to January 1st of the next year
• Seamlessly resets after midnight to begin counting down to the following New Year
• Lightweight and battery-friendly — runs entirely on your device with zero network usage
• Full HD (1920×1080) display optimized for modern Roku devices

**Privacy First:**
This screensaver collects absolutely no data. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no network connections. It simply reads your device's clock and displays the countdown. Safe for the whole family.

**How to Activate:**
After installing, go to Settings → Screensaver on your Roku device and select "New Year Countdown" as your screensaver. The countdown will automatically appear when your Roku is idle.

Whether you're planning a New Year's Eve party, looking for a festive screensaver during the holiday season, or just love watching time tick by — New Year Countdown adds a touch of anticipation to your TV screen all year long.

Free forever. No ads. No in-app purchases.
