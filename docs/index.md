# New Year Countdown Screensaver

A beautiful Roku screensaver that displays a live countdown to the New Year.

- [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy)
- [Terms of Use](terms-of-use)

## About

New Year Countdown is a free screensaver for Roku devices that shows a real-time countdown in **days : hours : minutes : seconds** until midnight on New Year's Eve. It features a sleek dark navy design with gold accents and a subtle sparkle animation.
